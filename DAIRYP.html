DAIRY PRODUCTS MAKE YOU FAT!


The regular consumption of dairy products in your daily diet is going to make you fat and gain more weight over time – not lose it. Dairy Products are not fattening, and what’s more is that they can have an opposite effect which has been proven through several studies.


The first study , and the one I found most interesting , was published in the International Journal of Obesity , where 14 clinical trials experimented on 883 adults in the age range of 18-85. The ideas behind the research was to see how dairy products affected the participant’s weight , muscles mass, and far. Researchers found that 3-4 servings of dairy products – combined with a low calorie diet – actually contributed to weight loss when compared to those adults who compeltely skipped all forms of dairy produce from their daily diet.
