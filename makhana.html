BENEFITS OF MAKHANA (FOX NUTS OR LOTUS SEEDS)


Makhana is a type of seed derived from the Euryale Ferox plant. Theyâ€™re also sometimes referred to as fox nuts or lotus Seeds. Makhanas are widely cultivated throughout Asia and often used in traditional forms of medicine to treat various conditions.

Makhana is an excellent source of several important nutrients and makes a great addition to a healthy, well rounded diet. It contains a good amount of carbs in each serving and is also rich in several micronutrients, including calcium, magnesium, iron and phosphorus.


Makhana is rich in various antioxidants, which are compounds that help neutralize harmful free radicals and prevent oxidation stress.


Adding makhana seeds to your diet is a great way to boost your intake of protein and fibre- two key nutrients that may benefit weight loss. Protein has been shown to reduce food cravings and help regulate your appetite. Meanwhile, fibre moves slowly through your digestive tract to keep you feeling full during the day.


One of the most popular ways to prepare makhana involves roasting the seeds and seasoning them with your choice of herbs and spices, such as sea salt, turmeric, chilli powder, or black pepper. It can be roasted and enjoyed as a savory snack or added to desserts.
